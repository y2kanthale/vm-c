Company Profile

V.M. Consultants a proprietary concern was founded in 1986. The firm has extended the structural consultancy services in all segments of construction industry including large residential, commercial, IT buildings malls, multiplexes, industrial projects and projects related to structural dynamics, machine foundations ,and lot more.
